Resistance Mechanism Analysis

Here we explore different hypotheses for the resistance mechanisms observed in a K. pneumoniae strain. The hypotheses are evaluated based on the observed resistance pattern, which includes high resistance to Pip Tazo and aztreonam, cefepime resistance, high resistance to ceftazidime, and resistance to carbapenems (especially ertapenem).

1. ESBL mediated resistance

Originally suspected due to patient history. However, the phenotype is now ESBL negative. Additionally, aztreonam resistance is present.
